# Nice Hotel Gym

Better-equipped hotel fitness center (luxury/business hotels).

## Equipment

### Cardio
- Treadmills: 3-5
- Bikes: 2-3
- Ellipticals: 2
- Rower: Sometimes

### Strength
- Dumbbells: Up to 75-100 lbs
- Adjustable bench: 2+
- Cable machine: Often yes
- Smith machine: Sometimes
- Leg press: Occasionally

### Other
- TRX/suspension trainer
- Kettlebells
- Medicine balls
- Foam rollers

## Workout Adaptations

### Upper Body Push
- DB Bench Press (heavy available)
- DB Incline Press
- Cable flyes
- DB Shoulder Press
- Dips (if station available)

### Upper Body Pull
- Cable rows
- DB Rows (heavy)
- Cable pulldowns
- Face pulls
- Curls (various)

### Lower Body
- Goblet squats (heavy)
- DB Romanian deadlifts
- Leg press (if available)
- Bulgarian split squats
- Leg curls (if machine)

### Core
- Cable crunches
- Hanging leg raises (if bar)
- Planks
- Ab wheel (sometimes available)

## Sample Workouts

### Push Day
1. DB Bench: 4x8
2. DB Incline Press: 3x10
3. DB Shoulder Press: 3x10
4. Cable Flyes: 3x12
5. Tricep Pushdowns: 3x12

### Pull Day
1. Cable Pulldowns: 4x10
2. DB Rows: 4x8 each
3. Cable Rows: 3x12
4. Face Pulls: 3x15
5. Curls: 3x12

### Legs
1. Goblet Squats: 4x10
2. DB RDL: 3x10
3. Leg Press: 3x12 (if available)
4. Walking Lunges: 3x12 each
5. Calf Raises: 3x15

## Notes
- Better weight selection allows more intensity
- May have attendant during certain hours
- Often has water/towels provided
- Usually 5am-10pm or 24hr
